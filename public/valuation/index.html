How to read this chart

The line is NVDA's forward P/E divided by the SOX forward P/E (cap-weighted harmonic of the 30 SOX constituents). A reading above 1.0 means NVDA trades at a premium to the index; below 1.0 means a discount. On Dec 19, 2025, Stacy Rasgon (Bernstein) flagged 0.87x as a 13% discount — only 13 days in ten years had been cheaper. The March 27, 2026 trough at 0.77x broke below all of them.

Data source: Yahoo Finance via yfinance, with manual overrides on top SOX weights when issuer data is more reliable. Historical anchors are reconstructed from the Rasgon/Bernstein Dec 2025 chart. This is research commentary, not investment advice.
