## What It Is

The Stack is a live, interactive dashboard tracking the unit economics of AI inference — from GPU silicon to API token pricing. Updated daily with real market data across 18 frontier models, 3 GPU markets, and 500+ cloud offerings.

## The Problem We Solve

Enterprise AI buyers, investors, and infrastructure teams lack real-time visibility into:
- What it actually costs to produce a token on different GPU classes
- Whether API providers are profitable or burning cash for market share
- How GPU hardware choice affects inference margins at different pricing tiers
- The true total cost of ownership beyond sticker-price GPU-hours

## What The Stack Includes
